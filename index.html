At Mang Harrison, we bring tradition to your table with our carefully crafted marinades. Whether you're preparing for a Sunday family lunch or a casual dinner, our marinated selections are infused with bold, home-style Filipino flavors that everyone will love.
From sweet and savory BBQ to classic adobo-style blends, each recipe is made to help you cook with ease and confidence—no need to prepare your own mix, just marinate, cook, and enjoy!

Our Specialties

At Mang Harrison, every dish is crafted with tradition, flavor, and heart. From our juicy chicken inasal and smoky BBQ to our flavorful inasal sa gata and signature seafood platters, each bite tells a story of rich Filipino taste.

Chicken Inasal Thigh

Marinated chicken thigh grilled to smoky, juicy perfection.

Seafood Platter

A generous mix of grilled shrimp, squid, and fish with dipping sauces.

Chicken Breast Inasal

Lean chicken breast soaked in inasal marinade and chargrilled tender.

Pork Sisig

Crispy, sizzling pork sisig served with onions, chili, and calamansi.
